CE Tours offers students an opportunity to visit and explore American history and Culture in Washington, DC and New York. History teachers and families can register their students on this educational trip to learn with students from around the country. The trip begins as students arrive in Washington, D.C. to get acquainted with other participants during an afternoon of fun and social activities. Over the following 5 days, students will explore numerous activities, historic sites, and attractions related to American history and culture. This trip will provide students with an opportunity to experience U.S. history and government first-hand and on a whole new level. The cost of this all inclusive tour is $1955.00 per person and it will be led by our staff, as well as local high school and middle school social Studies teachers. Register for this trip to Washington DC and New York City today using the login ID “97216212″ from the homepage.
